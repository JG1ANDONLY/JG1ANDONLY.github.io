Causal Effect of Type II Diabetes on Prostate Cancer in the East Asian Population
I performed two-sample Mendelian randomization with the inverse variance weighted method while using MR Egger and weighted median methods as sensitivity analysis on genetic-level data.

In this project, I identified proxy SNPs in linkage disequilibrium (r2 > 0.8) and obtained OR = 0.76, 95% CI = [0.76, 0.89], P-value = 2.26 * 10-6 and similar results in sensitivity analysis. We reached an conclusion that type II diabetes has a negative causal effect on prostate cancer using genetic evidence
