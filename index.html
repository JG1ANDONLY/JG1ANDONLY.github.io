Joined the PsychENCODE project at Prof. Anshul Kundaje's lab. Analyzed scRNA-seq and scATAC-seq multiome data from 3 brain regions to investigate cell-type-specific chromatin accessibility and gene expression patterns underlying schizophrenia (SCZ) and bipolar disorder (BPD).

  • Pre-processed multiome data: QC, dimensionality reduction, cell clustering, doublet removal, and marker gene detection.
  • Ran differential gene expression & peak accessibility analyses (pseudobulk DESeq2; single-cell Wilcoxon + MAST) across cell types stratified by sex and disease.
  • Cross-referencing ChromBPNet outputs to identify causal disease variants and their downstream effects on gene regulation.

Computationally Investigating Black–White Metabolomic Disparities in Prostate Cancer
LC-MS Metabolomics ChemRICH WGCNA MetaboAnalystR limma R

Figure Source: NCI 2019  ·  Cancer Letters 2022

Click to expand ↓

Black men face disproportionately higher prostate cancer incidence and mortality. This project leverages LC-MS metabolomics on a cohort of 34 men (17 Black, 17 White) from the IRONMAN Registry to uncover underlying metabolomic disparities. Manuscript in preparation — also my Master's thesis.

  • Applied ChemRICH (three custom methods), PCA, PLS-DA, Random Forest, SVM, and logistic regression to identify key contributing metabolites.
  • Built WGCNA co-expression networks and used limma differential analysis to isolate aging-influenced metabolites.
